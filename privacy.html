4. Third-Party Data Sources

The App aggregates publicly available data from third-party platforms to generate stock sentiment analysis. These sources include:

  • Reddit (old.reddit.com): Publicly accessible posts and comments from investment-related subreddits. We do not access private Reddit data or require Reddit credentials.
  • StockTwits: Public message streams and trending symbol data via the StockTwits public API.
  • Yahoo Finance (via yfinance): Publicly available stock price and volume data.
  • Anthropic Claude API: We send anonymized ticker data and publicly scraped text to Claude for AI-based sentiment analysis. No personally identifiable information is included in these requests.

We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Reddit, StockTwits, Yahoo Finance, or Anthropic.
